# Viewing Clips

The **Clips** page is where you spend most of your time enjoying the media collected from your server.

## Navigating the Viewer

- **Guild Selection**: If you are in multiple servers using Guild Moments, click the server icon at the top to switch between them.
- **Channel Filters**: Use the channel dropdown to narrow down clips to specific channels.
- **Search**: The search bar looks through message content, filenames, and clip titles.

## The Clip Player

When you click on a clip, it opens in a theater mode player.
- **Autoplay**: Clips will autoplay muted by default. Adjusting the volume will save your preference for future clips.
- **Continuous Playback**: Scrolling down in the modal allows you to quickly move to the next or previous clips.
- **Metadata**: You can see who posted the clip, what they said, and when it was posted.

## Managing Clips

While viewing a clip, you can perform several actions (if you have the right permissions):
- **Favorite**: Click the heart icon to save it to your personal favorites list (`/clips?favorites=true`).
- **Edit Title**: Give the clip a memorable title.
- **Add Tags**: Apply server-defined tags to categorize the clip.
- **Visibility**: Hide a clip from the public feed without deleting it entirely.
- **Delete**: Permanently remove the clip from the database.
